## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

With the increasing use of digital payments and online platforms,
it is important for students to stay alert and follow safe practices.

### General Safety Tips

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone  
- Always verify the identity of unknown callers or messages  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files  
- Use only official and trusted apps for payments and communication  
- Keep your phone and apps updated with the latest security updates  
- Enable screen lock, app lock, or biometric security on your device  
- Regularly check your bank account and transaction history  
- Log out from devices that are shared or not personally owned  

### UPI and Financial Safety Tips

- Remember: entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money  
- Never approve “collect requests” from unknown people  
- Do not scan QR codes to receive money  
- Double-check the receiver’s details before making any payment  

### Reporting Cybercrime

If you become a victim of cyber fraud, report it immediately:

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Cyber Crime Helpline Number: 1930  

Taking quick action can increase the chances of recovering lost money.
